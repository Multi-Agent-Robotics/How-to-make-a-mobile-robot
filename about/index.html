Assembling the Robot

  1. The frame
  2. Saw the laths to length.
  3. Using angle brackets and the 4 frame pieces, assemble the frame.
  4. Saw the board to size.
  5. With screws attach the board as flooring to the frame.
  6. Drivetrain
  7. Follow instructions from Flipsky on how to assemble the drivetrain.
