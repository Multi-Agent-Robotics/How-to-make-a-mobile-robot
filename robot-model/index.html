Robot model

This contains information and 3d models of the robot we are about to build.

  1. Wheels: All Terrain Honeycomb Wheels
  2. Size:6'' ATM d=150mm, w=50mm
  3. Wheel bearing:608RS
  4. Pulley bearing:6900RS
  5. Pulley Teeth: 60T
  6. Belt:335mm, 15mm Width

  7. Material:Nylon

  8. Motors: Flipsky BLDC Belt Motor Battle Hardened 6374

  9. KV: 190KV
  10. Max Power: 3500Watts
  11. Max Current: 85 Amps
  12. Max Volts: 12S 
  13. Max Torque: 8Nm
  14. Motor Resistance: 0.05Ohm
  15. Recommend ESC: 3-12S 50A (FSESC4.12 50A or FSESC 6.6 60A)
  16. The number of pole: 14
  17. Net weight: 980g
  18. Motor wire:12AWG

  19. Shaft size : 10mm

  20. Battery: 6-21S 100A UART BT, Li-ion.

  21. 13 Cells (11 in series, 2 in parrallel)
  22. 5.0 Ah
  23. small blog on the battery components
  24. Battery management system: JBD Smart BMS 6~21S
